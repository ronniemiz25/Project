Provides Protection

Insurance coverage does reduce the impact of loss that one bears in perilous situations.It provides monetary reimbursement during financial crises. It not only protects the insured from financial woes but also mental stress arising from it.

Provides Certainty

Insurance coverage provides a feeling of assurance to the policyholders. The insured pays a small portion of the income for this certainty that will help in the future. So, there is a certainty of handsome financial aid against the premium.

Risk Sharing

The very manner in which insurance policy functions makes it a cooperative scheme. An insurer would be unable to pay from one’s capital. An insurance company pools in collective risks and premiums because it covers a large number of risk-exposed people.

Value of Risk

Insurance policy assesses the volume of risk and anticipates the various causes of it. It evaluates the amount for insurance coverage and the premium payment amounts on a risk value basis and protects against unforeseen consequential loss.
